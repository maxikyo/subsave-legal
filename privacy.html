3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes only:

  • Provide core functionality: Tracking and managing your subscriptions on your device.
  • Cloud backup and sync: Syncing your subscription data across devices via your account.
  • Subscription detection: Analyzing bank transactions to automatically identify recurring payments.
  • Premium access: Verifying your subscription status to unlock paid features.
  • Security: Rate limiting API requests to prevent abuse.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Bank account connections — you explicitly authorize read-only access to your transaction data. You may withdraw this consent at any time by disconnecting the bank in the app.
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Premium subscription management — processing purchase data to deliver paid features you have purchased.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): Core app functionality — processing local subscription data to provide the tracking service you requested. Our legitimate interest is delivering a functional app; this is balanced against your rights as the processing is minimal and expected.

5. PSD2 Financial Data Processing

SubSave accesses bank account data through PSD2-compliant services. This section details how financial data is handled:

  • Access type: Read-only. SubSave cannot initiate payments, transfers, or modify your bank account in any way.
  • Purpose: Transaction data is analyzed solely to detect recurring payments and identify subscriptions.
  • Processing: Transactions are processed in the app's memory. Raw transaction data is not stored on our servers.
  • Sharing: Financial data is never shared with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.
  • EnableBanking (EU): Connections comply with PSD2 regulation. Consent is valid for 90 days and can be revoked at any time through the app or directly with EnableBanking.
  • Monobank (UA): Access uses your personal API token, which is stored encrypted (AES-GCM) on our servers and can be deleted at any time through the app settings.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed and stored within the European Union. Where data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • RevenueCat (United States): Transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) adopted by the European Commission, in accordance with GDPR Art. 46(2)(c). RevenueCat maintains a Data Processing Agreement that includes these clauses.
  • Supabase, EnableBanking, Upstash (EU): Data remains within the EU/EEA. No international transfer safeguards are required.
  • Monobank (Ukraine): Your API token is stored encrypted in our EU-based infrastructure. Communication with Monobank's API occurs directly from your device.

8. Data Retention

  • Local data: Persists on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. We have no access to locally stored data.
  • Cloud backup: Retained while your account exists. Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Bank connection data: EnableBanking consent expires automatically after 90 days. Monobank tokens are deleted immediately when you disconnect.
  • Transaction data: Processed in real-time for subscription detection. Raw transactions are not retained on our servers.
  • Account data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Purchase data: Retained by RevenueCat for the duration required to manage your subscription and comply with applicable tax and financial regulations.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR (Art. 15-22)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (e.g., JSON).
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to restrict processing (Art. 18): Request limitation of data processing.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., disconnect bank accounts). Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. In the Czech Republic, this is the Office for Personal Data Protection (UOOU).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at subsave.support@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

10. Your Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with additional rights:

  • Right to know: You may request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources, the business purpose for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt-out of sale or sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Because we do not engage in these activities, there is no need to opt out.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise your rights, email subsave.support@gmail.com. We will verify your identity before processing your request and respond within 45 days.

Categories of personal information collected in the past 12 months: Identifiers (email address), commercial information (subscription and purchase data), financial information (bank transactions processed temporarily for subscription detection). 11. Data Deletion

You can delete your data through the following methods:

  • Account deletion: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all cloud data including backups, stored tokens, and account information within 30 days.
  • Bank disconnection: Go to Settings > Bank Connections and revoke any connected bank. This immediately deletes stored tokens and consent data.
  • App uninstall: Removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Email request: Contact subsave.support@gmail.com to request deletion of all data associated with your account.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by GDPR Art. 33.
  • Notify affected users without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms, as required by GDPR Art. 34.
  • Provide details about the nature of the breach, the data affected, the likely consequences, and the measures taken to address it.

14. Cookies, Tracking, and Do Not Track

SubSave does not use tracking cookies, advertising identifiers, or analytics SDKs. We do not track your behavior across apps or websites. No data is shared with advertisers or data brokers.

Do Not Track (DNT): Some web browsers transmit "Do Not Track" signals. Since SubSave does not track users across third-party websites or services, our practices remain the same regardless of whether a DNT signal is received.

15. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS).
  • Monobank tokens are encrypted with AES-GCM before storage.
  • Supabase uses Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure strict isolation of user data.
  • API endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse.
  • Authentication uses industry-standard JWT tokens with Supabase Auth.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; we never store plaintext passwords.
